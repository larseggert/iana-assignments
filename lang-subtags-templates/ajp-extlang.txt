This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-3 effective
2023-01-20, merging 'ajp' for South Levantine Arabic into 'apc' and
changing the reference name for 'apc' from "North Levantine Arabic" to
"Levantine Arabic". Both the primary language subtag and the
corresponding extended language subtag were updated for each ISO 639-3
code element.

For more information on the ISO 639-3 change, refer to:
https://iso639-3.sil.org/request/2022-006
