5. Reference to published description of the language (book or article):

The publications "Soziophonologische Variation im gegenwärtigen Wiener
Deutsch" (1987) by Sylvia Moosmüller, "Dialect use as interaction
strategy: a sociolinguistic study of contextualization, speech
perception, and language attitudes in Austria" (2009) by Barbara Soukup,
'"Grüss Gott!": A Study of Austrian Identity Through Language' (2011) by
Katherine Portnoy, and "Regional and National Identity in Austrian
Dialectal Pop Songs" (2014) by Ulrike Thumberger all discuss the
specific differences of Viennese German from other Austrian dialects and
its cultural significance when compared to other dialects.
