This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639-1 (now ISO 639 Set 1)
effective 2021-05-25, deprecating [bh] for "Bihari languages". The
alpha-3 identifier [bih] is still present in Sets 2 and 5 and is
recommended by ISO 639 as a replacement for [bh].

For more information on the ISO 639 change, refer to:
https://www.loc.gov/standards/iso639-2/php/code_changes.php
