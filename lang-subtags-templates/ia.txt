This registration tracks a change made to ISO 639 effective 2026-04-15,
adding "Interlingua (IALA)" as the reference name for [ina] (which is
represented by ISO 639 Set 1 [ia] in the Language Subtag Registry) and
retaining "Interlingua (International Auxiliary Language Association)"
as an additional name.

For more information on the ISO 639 change, refer to:
