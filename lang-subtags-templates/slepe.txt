4. Intended meaning of the subtag:

The Sorbian dialect of Schleife is one of the Sorbian transitional
dialects between Upper Sorbian (hsb) and Lower Sorbian (dsb), but nearer
to Lower Sorbian. The native name of this dialect is "Slěpjańšćina"; the
subtag value is derived from the short form "Slěpe."
