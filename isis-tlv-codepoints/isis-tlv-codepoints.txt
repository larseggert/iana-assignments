   Registration Procedure(s)

 Expert Review

   Expert(s)

   Description

 This registry defines sub-TLVs for TLVs advertising neighbor information.
 The list of TLVs includes:

 Extended IS reachability (22)
 IS Neighbor Attribute (23)
 L2 Bundle Member Attributes (25)
 inter-AS reachability information (141)
 MT-ISN (222)
 MT IS Neighbor Attribute (223)

   Reference
           [RFC5305][RFC5316][RFC7370][RFC8668]

   Note

 Allocations within this registry require documentation of the use of
 the allocated value and approval by the Designated Expert assigned by
 the IESG.


   Note

 The column for TLV 25 may be marked as follows:

 y - sub-TLV may appear in TLV 25 but MUST NOT be shared by multiple L2 Bundle Members
 y(s) - sub-TLV may appear in TLV 25 and MAY be shared by multiple L2 Bundle Members
 n - sub-TLV MUST NOT appear in TLV 25
