   Registration Procedure(s)

 New values must not be directly added to the "iana-ospf-functional-cap-bits"
 YANG module. They must instead be added to the "OSPF Router Functional
 Capability Bits" registry in the [Open Shortest Path First (OSPF) Parameters]
 registry group.
