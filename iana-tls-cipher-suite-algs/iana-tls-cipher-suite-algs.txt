   Description

 New values must not be directly added to the
 "iana-tls-cipher-suite-algs" YANG module. They must instead be added
 to the "TLS Cipher Suites" registry in the "Transport Layer Security
 (TLS) Parameters" registry group [IANA registry tls-parameters].
