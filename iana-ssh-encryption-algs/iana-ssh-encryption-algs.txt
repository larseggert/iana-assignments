   Description

 New values must not be directly added to the "iana-ssh-encryption-algs"
 YANG module. They must instead be added to the "Encryption Algorithm Names"
 registry in the "Secure Shell (SSH) Protocol Parameters" registry group
 [IANA registry ssh-parameters].
